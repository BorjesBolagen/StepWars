# Privacy Policy for Stega

*Last updated: July 7, 2026*

Stega is a step-competition app where you compete with your steps against
friends and other users. This policy describes what personal data the app
processes, why, and your rights.

**Data controller:** Börjesbolagen
**Contact:** johan@borjeskoncernen.se

## Data we process

| Data | Purpose |
| ---- | ------- |
| Email address | Sign-in and password reset |
| Display name | Shown to other users in leaderboards and challenges |
| Birth year | Places you in the right age group — we never store full date of birth or national ID |
| Daily step totals (health data) | The core of the app: leaderboards, challenges and milestones |
| Friendships and challenges | So you can compete with your friends |
| Push notification token | Notifications about challenges, friend requests and results |

## Health data — important

- Stega reads **step counts only**, via Health Connect (Android) or the
  phone's pedometer (iOS) — no heart rate, location, weight or any other
  health information.
- Only **daily totals** are stored (e.g. "8,432 steps on July 5"), never
  raw sensor data, timestamps or movement patterns.
- Your step data is visible only to you, your accepted friends, and
  participants of challenges you have joined. Public leaderboards show
  only name, age group and step total.
- Health data is **never sold, never shared with third parties for
  marketing, and never used for advertising**.
- You can deny or revoke the step permission at any time in your phone's
  settings; the app then runs in a limited mode.

## Legal basis

Processing is based on **contract** (delivering the service you signed up
for) and **consent** (health data and push notifications — both request
explicit approval in the app and can be revoked).

## Storage and recipients

Data is stored with **Supabase** (database and authentication provider)
in an EU region. Push notifications are delivered via **Expo**
(Expo.dev) — only the notification token and message text, never health
data. There are no other recipients; we never sell data.

## Retention and deletion

Data is kept until you delete your account. You can delete it directly in
the app: **Profile → Delete account and all data** — this permanently and
immediately removes your account, step data, friendships, challenges and
notification tokens. You can also request deletion via
johan@borjeskoncernen.se.

## Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify and erase your data, the right to
data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with your data
protection authority (in Sweden: IMY). Contact us at the address above.

## Age limit

Stega is intended for users aged 13 or older.

## Changes

We will announce material changes in the app. The latest version is
always available on this page.
